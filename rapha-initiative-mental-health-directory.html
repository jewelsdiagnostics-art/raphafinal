Emergency and Immediate Support

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or go to the nearest hospital. For domestic violence-related support in Ghana, the Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection Toll Free Helplines listed in this document are:

0800 800 800 / 0800 900 900

Most tertiary, regional and district hospitals may have some of the following professionals on staff:

  • Specialist Physician Assistants in Psychiatry
  • Registered Mental Health Nurses
  • Community Mental Health Officers

For information about licensing and accreditation, please contact the Health Facilities Regulatory Agency, the Ghana Medical and Dental Council, the Ghana Psychological Council or the Nurses and Midwifery Council.
